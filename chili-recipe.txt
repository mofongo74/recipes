Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds jackfruit
 - 1 large white onion, diced
 - 2 tablespoons of olive oil
 - 1 1/2 teaspoons of chili oil
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - One 8-ounce can tomato sauce
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
 - 1/4 cup corn flour
 - One 15-ounce can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
 - One 15-ounce can pinto beans, drained and rinsed

Directions:
 1. Core and peel a jackfruit. Place fruit into a saucepan on medium 
    low with oil and onion until tender.
 2. Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, oregano, salt, nutmeg, cinnamon and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then cover and simmer over low heat for one hour.
 4. Mix the corn flour and 1/2 cup water in a small bowl.  Add to chili.
 5. Add the beans and simmer for 20 more minutes.
